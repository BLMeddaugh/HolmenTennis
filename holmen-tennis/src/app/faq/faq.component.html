How do I register to play on the team?

  • The player will need a physical on file with the school, which can be found here.
  • The player will also need to turn in a registration form found here.

    What do I need to play?

  • Racket- These can be bought locally at somewhere like Dicks Sporting Goods or Walmart, however online retailers like Tennis Warehouse, Tennis Express or tennis-point, have a much better selection. They also have a demo progam so the player could try out several rackets to find one they like.
  • Water Bottle- Please bring one large enough to get through a match without needing refills, as that is not always possible.
  • Uniform- These are ordered every year by the team in the first few weeks.
  • Shorts/Tennis Skirts- Please have shorts that don't clash with the school colors too badly, and they MUST have somewhere to store at least one tennis ball.
  • Sweater- We do have a team sweater, you will most likely want a sweater or jacket as it's not always nice out!

    Does Holmen Highschool have tennis courts?

  • It doesn't. Unfortunately, our four courts are located at Viking Elementary School.

    How does my player get to there after school?

  • Historically players drive, give each other rides there, or they can take a nearby school bus after they let a coach know.

    Can I fix my broken tennis racket?

  • We are able to get rackets restrung, and usually fix grommets at a minimal cost. Please talk to the coaches for more info on this.
  • A racket which is developing cracks that is not repairable.

    What do we do if the weather is bad?

  • For pratice days, we are often able to get gym time in the new Gym D where we have a full size tennis court. Please be prepared to play inside on bad weather days, and bring your gear to school.
  • Match days are often either delayed or rescheduled. The coaches will do our best to keep you updated, however these calls are often made day of; a few hours before.
